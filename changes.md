# News and changes

## Jan 2026

### 18th Jan 2026
Finished working with PNG and Alpha-color supports.  
LOCATE in graphical screen now overdraws old text instead of just writing new top of it.  

**Supported Formats**

|Format|Transparency|Notes|
|------|-----------|-------------|
|PNG|Full alpha (0-255)|Recommended for sprites|
|BMP|None|Legacy support|
